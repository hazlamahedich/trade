Project Portfolio

Counterpoint

Transparent AI Reasoning for Traders.
The first platform to visualize the "Why" behind market moves.

The Problem

Black-Box Confusion

Trading signals today are opaque. Users receive a "BUY" alert without context, leading to blind following and inevitable losses. There is no learning, only dependency.

  • ✕ No Explanation
  • ✕ No Risk Context
  • ✕ "Trust Me Bro" Culture

The Solution

Transparent Debate

Counterpoint replaces blind signals with a live debate between reasoning agents. Users watch the logic unfold, understand the risks, and make their own informed decisions.

  • ✓ Logic in Plain English
  • ✓ Real-Time Risk Checks
  • ✓ Confidence Scores

The Architecture of Reasoning

At the core of Counterpoint is a proprietary 3-Agent Debate System designed to mimic the internal monologue of a professional trader.

DEBATE
The Bull
Analyzes upside potential.
"Why should we buy?"
The Bear
Identifies downside risks.
"Why might this fail?"
Risk Guardian
The Adult in the Room.
Checks volatility & exposure.

The Result: The Verdict. A synthesized, logic-backed recommendation with clear Entry, Exit, and Stop-Loss levels.

Technical Implementation & Innovation

Counterpoint isn't just a UI wrapper; it is a sophisticated orchestration of multiple LLM agents working in parallel.

➜
Orchestration Layer

Manages the debate protocol, ensuring agents stay on topic and adhere to strict financial reasoning constraints.

➜
Real-Time Data Integration

Agents analyze live market feeds (Price, Volume, RSI) to ground their arguments in factual reality.

➜
Customizable Strategy Injection

Users can "teach" the agents their own rules (e.g., "Argue based on RSI divergence"), making the platform highly personalized.

➜
Visual Confidence Engine

Proprietary algorithm converts qualitative arguments into a quantitative "Traffic Light" confidence score.
